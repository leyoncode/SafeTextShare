Welcome to SafeTextShare. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our temporary text sharing platform. By accessing or using SafeTextShare, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with these terms, please do not use our service.

1. Information We Collect

When you use SafeTextShare, we may collect the following information:

  • Text Data: The text content you share on SafeTextShare. This text is stored temporarily and automatically deleted after 3 days.
  • IP Address: The IP address of the device used to share the text. We collect this information to maintain the security and integrity of our service. IP addresses are stored temporarily alongside the text data and are deleted after 3 days.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our temporary text sharing service.
  • To ensure the security and integrity of our platform, including monitoring for potential abuses or violations of our terms.
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve user experience.

3. Sharing of Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • To comply with legal obligations, such as responding to a subpoena, court order, or other legal process.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of SafeTextShare, our users, or the public.
  • If we undergo a merger, acquisition, or any form of sale of some or all of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of that business transaction. We will provide notice before your personal information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

SafeTextShare is a temporary text sharing platform. We retain text data and associated IP addresses for a period of 3 days. After this period, the data is automatically deleted from our servers. We do not retain any personal information beyond this time frame.

5. Security of Your Information

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

SafeTextShare does not use cookies or other tracking technologies to collect personal information. Our platform is designed to provide a simple, temporary text sharing service without requiring additional data collection.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party sites. Please review the privacy policies of any third-party websites you visit.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you by posting a notice on our website. Your continued use of SafeTextShare after any changes to this Privacy Policy will constitute your acknowledgment of the changes and your consent to abide by and be bound by the modified Privacy Policy.
